Nkurunungi Nyansio Donnah, Nsabe Silas and Gumisiriza Naboth were forwarded to fort portal court after being arrested for illegal possession of wildlife products to wit 03 bundles of walking sticks of Teclea nobilis on the 27/08/2015. they pleaded guilty and were sentenced to 02 months of community service (Naboth and Silas) and caution respectively. Silas dodged the sentence and he was re arrested on 13/11/2015 and sentenced to 06 months imprisonment and the file was closed.
